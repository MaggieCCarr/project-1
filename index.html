Slutty Brownies

Prep Time:
20 minutes
Bake Time:
30-40 minutes

Slutty Brownies are one of my favorite things to bake with my friends. They are easy to make and so delicious. They are the best out of the oven served with a little vanilla ice cream!

Ingredients

  • cookie dough
  • Oreos™
  • brownie mix
  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup water
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix brownie mix, eggs, water, and vegetable oil in a bowl
  3. Spray pan with nonstick spray
  4. Press layer of cookie dough in bottom of pan
  5. Place oreos on top of cookie dough
  6. Pour mixed brownie batter over cookie dough and oreos
  7. Let bake for 30 to 40 minutes
  8. Let cool and slice brownies
  9. Enjoy!
